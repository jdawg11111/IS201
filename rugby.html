Rugby

I started playing rugby when I was 12 years old and fell in love with the sport. It's the perfect combination of physicality, fitness, and skill. I excelled in the sport growing up and played on the varsity club team as our team captain. Our team won several tournaments and state championships.

My coaches had a big impact on me and the person I've become. The coach in the picture above is from South Africa. He taught me grit, respect, and the importance of family.

Why Rugby is an Awesome Sport

  1. Physical Fitness
    • Enhances cardiovascular health
    • Builds strength and endurance
  2. Teamwork
    • Fosters strong communication skills
    • Develops trust among teammates
  3. Strategic Thinking
    • Encourages quick decision-making
    • Promotes adaptability on the field
